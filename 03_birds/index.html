How can Augmented Reality (AR) technology enhance the learning process of people?

How are the applications using AR technology different from others from the perspective of UX design?

What is the role of AR in the process of communicating information?

The Birds on Earth is my first try to design and prototype an application using Augmented Reality (AR) technology. It is a handheld mobile video see-through augmented reality based on a series of images (a self-designed children book) as targets.

With the curiosity of this emerging technology and the three questions in mind, I finally decided to start from a specific project to learn designing with AR: an AR application that can help kids learn about birds.
